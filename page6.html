Man “High As A Kite” Tries To Steal Monkey From Zoo For Girlfriend

Guy tries to steal monkey for his girlfriend in New Zealand at wellington zoo. John Owen Casford claims he was “high as a kite” when he broke into the zoo at night to attempting to steal the monkey for his girlfriend. He enters the zoo through an unsecured gate and broke through 2 padlocks to enter the animals’ enclosure. One monkey had a hematoma and others had scratch marks on them. Casford was sentenced to 2 years and 7 months in prison.

Judge Rules 30-Year-Old Must Finally Move Out Of His Parents’ House

Even after five notices and an offer of cash to move out of his parents’ home in Camillus, N.Y., Michael Rotondo pulled a real-life Failure to Launch and refused to leave. So, his parents did what every loving mother and father at the end of their rope would do. They took him to court. Rotondo asked his parents for 30 days’ notice so he can leave the premise but his parents disagree but he would only leave after 30 days so he can have time to pack up so his parents took him to court. He failed and have to move out of his parents’ house immediately.
